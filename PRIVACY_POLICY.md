# Privacy Policy — Knips

**Effective date:** 9 July 2026

## Summary

Knips does not collect, store, transmit, or sell any of your personal data. The
app has no accounts, no analytics, no advertising, and no backend servers operated
by us. Everything the app does stays between your device and the servers **you**
choose to connect it to.

## Who we are

Knips ("the app") is developed and published by Andrea Lapiana, an individual
developer. If you have any questions about this policy, contact:
**andrealap2000@gmail.com**.

## What Knips is

Knips is a **client** for music servers that you host or control yourself
(Navidrome / OpenSubsonic servers, and optionally a File Browser instance for
playlist editing). It is not a music service and provides no content of its own.

## Information the app handles

**Server details you enter.** To connect to your server, you provide a server
address, username, password, and (optionally) access headers such as a Cloudflare
Access token. This information is stored **only on your device** — passwords and
secrets in the iOS Keychain, other settings in local app storage. It is used
solely to connect to the server you specified. It is **never sent to us** or to
any third party.

**Network communication.** The app communicates only with the server addresses you
configure. We operate no servers that your app talks to.

**Trial and purchase status.** So the 7‑day free trial and one‑time unlock work
correctly, the app stores a trial start date and your unlock status on your device
and in your **own private iCloud** (via iCloud Key‑Value Storage). This information
stays within your Apple account and is **not accessible to us**. The purchase
itself is processed by **Apple**; we never see your payment details. Apple's
handling of purchase data is governed by Apple's own privacy policy.

**No analytics or tracking.** Knips contains no analytics SDKs, no advertising
identifiers (IDFA), no third‑party trackers, and no crash‑reporting services that
identify you.

## Children

Knips is not directed to children and does not knowingly handle any data from
children.

## Your rights

Because we do not collect or hold any personal data about you, there is nothing for
us to access, correct, export, or delete on our side. Data stored on your device is
removed when you delete the app. (Note: the trial start date kept in your private
iCloud may persist in your iCloud account; you can remove it via your iCloud
settings.) If you are in the EU/EEA, you retain your rights under the GDPR; contact
us at the address above with any request.
